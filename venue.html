Venue

Russian-Armenian University

The conference will be held at the Russian-Armenian University (RAU), located in Yerevan, Armenia.

Location

123 Hovsep Emin Street
0051 Yerevan, Armenia

About Russian-Armenian University

Russian-Armenian University offers a unique blend of Russian and Armenian educational traditions. It provides undergraduate, graduate, and postgraduate degrees in various fields.

Facilities

  • Modern Auditoriums and Lecture Halls
  • High-Speed Wi-Fi
  • Library and Research Centers
  • Cafeteria and Lounge Areas

Getting There

The university is accessible by public transportation and has ample parking for those who prefer to drive.

Accommodation

There are several hotels and guesthouses near the university for your convenience.
