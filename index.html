About NanoPQIQO 2024

Mission Statement

Our conference serves a dual purpose: to explore the latest advancements in nanomaterials, photonics, quantum optics, and quantum information, while also emphasizing inclusivity and participation from researchers and students from developing countries.

Scope

  • Synthesis and characterization of advanced nanomaterials
  • Advanced materials for photonic applications
  • Plasmonic nanomaterials for enhanced light-matter interactions in photonics
  • Quantum properties of nanomaterials and their impact on optical systems
  • Quantum dots and nanocrystals for quantum optical devices
  • Nanomaterial-based sensors and detectors
  • Nanophotonics and nanoscale optics
  • Quantum information processing and communication using nanomaterials
  • Quantum entanglement and coherence in nanomaterial systems
  • Hybrid nanomaterial platforms for quantum computing and simulation
  • Quantum sensing and imaging with nanomaterials
  • Quantum-inspired algorithms and machine learning with nanomaterials
  • Applications of nanomaterials in quantum cryptography and secure communication
